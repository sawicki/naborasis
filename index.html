Instructions for use

Overview

  • The app allows you see what dates the NABOR ASIS contract deadlines are due given the contract effective date and the number of days until these deadlines. This app is intended to give you a quick overview and all dates need to be checked and verified
  • Most dates are based on the contract execution date so that date should be select first
  • Since the contract specifies deadlines roll to the next business day, all computed weekend dates will roll to the following Monday
  • This app has no knowledge of federal holidays so all dates need to adjusted for their occurance. The official govermant list can be found here
